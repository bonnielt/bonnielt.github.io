Apprenticeship @Service Design Studio

Background

I joined the Service Design Studio at the Mayor's Office for Economic Opportunity in October 2018. My role as a Service Design Apprentice is to support various studio activities and needs– some verbs to describe my work are: 
‍
- listening to stakeholders
- making sense of research data
- visualizing learnings
- planning research strategy
‍- prototyping concepts to probe and explore
- observing how design operates in a government context

On the service design side...

One major area of contribution was creating design research artifacts to inform project strategy and documentation. Examples include creating the stakeholder map, service blueprint, and personas for Pathways to Prevention– our partnership with the Administration of Children's Services.

On the visual design side...

I also supported the Studio in designing some of our public-facing materials, such as stickers, e-mail headers, newsletters, and social media graphics.
