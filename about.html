About
I am a visual and experience designer with a focus on creating interactive experiences in both digital and physical spaces. My objective is to use design and participatory methods in order to create means for others to take action in their community. I am most enthusiastic about guiding creative direction and visualizing complex ideas in order to engage larger audiences. As a student design researcher in the Tactile and Tactical Lab, I was involved in cycles of rapid digital and physical prototyping.

I'm available for freelance projects in the branding, design and experience design space.
