Role and accomplishments

I was a UX designer and researcher in a team of 4. This project was awarded for Excellence in Design @HCDE Capstone 2018.

About

From March to June 2018, our team partnered with Seattle Housing Authority (SHA) to explore communication strategies regarding service provision among SHA staff members, service providers, and Yesler Terrace residents. Given the community’s diverse character, certain challenges arise when it comes to disseminating information among this network. Our task for 10 weeks was to investigate design opportunities to support access and awareness of resources.
